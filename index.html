About the app

BMI Calculator Shiny App
A web application to calculate and visualize BMI for better health monitoring.

Problem Statement

Many individuals are unaware of their BMI or how it correlates to health risks.
- Obesity and malnutrition are global health concerns.
- Lack of easy-to-use tools for calculating and interpreting BMI.

How the App Works

  • The app allows users to input their weight and height.
  • It calculates their BMI using the standard formula: BMI = weight(kg) / height(m)^2.
  • A visualization is generated showing the BMI value relative to a normal distribution.
  • Users can see if their BMI falls in a healthy range or indicates underweight/overweight status.

Key Benefits

  • Easy-to-Use: Simple input fields for weight and height.
  • Instant Feedback: Shows BMI and a graphical representation.
  • Health Awareness: Provides instant insight into whether the user’s BMI is within a healthy range, supporting better lifestyle choices.
  • Visual Data: Displays the BMI value on a distribution curve to enhance understanding.
